Authentic Flavors Since 2018

Founded by Alauddin A, Zara Caterers brings together traditional/signatured recipes with modern presentation. We specialize in authentic firewood biriyani with a focus on style of zeeraga samba biriyani and basmati biriyani ,and in civil of full veg thalis that celebrate regional diversity.

Our commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients ensures every dish bursts with authentic flavor. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, we craft memorable culinary experiences.
